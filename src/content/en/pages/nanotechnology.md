## SSR (Solar Self-Cleaning Reflex) Technology

Materix SSR is an innovative nano coating technology specifically developed for solar panels. Using advanced nanotechnology, SSR creates a self-cleaning, protective layer that significantly improves panel performance.

---

## How It Works

### Nano-Scale Protection
SSR coating creates an ultra-thin (nanometer-scale) protective film on the panel surface. This film has unique properties:

- **Hydrophobic (Water-Repellent):** Water beads up and rolls off, carrying dust and dirt with it
- **Anti-Static:** Reduces dust accumulation by minimizing static charge
- **UV Resistant:** Protects against UV degradation
- **Anti-Reflective:** Improves light transmission to the solar cells
