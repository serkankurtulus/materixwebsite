# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

At MATERIX Nano Technology Inc. ("MATERIX", "we", "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- Name and surname
- Email address
- Phone number
- Company name and title
- Address information
- Messages sent through contact forms

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Page view data
- Cookie data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- To provide and improve our services
- To respond to your inquiries and requests
- To manage quote and order processes
- To send marketing communications (with your consent)
- To analyze and improve our website
- To fulfill our legal obligations

## 3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following cases:

- When we have your explicit consent
- With our service providers (hosting, email services, etc.)
- When required by law
- In case of company merger or acquisition

## 4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data:

- SSL encryption
- Secure server infrastructure
- Access control and authorization
- Regular security audits

## 6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- Right to access your personal data
- Right to request correction of data
- Right to request deletion of data
- Right to object to data processing
- Right to data portability
