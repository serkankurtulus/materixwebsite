## Feature Details

### Water and Oil Repellency
High water and oil repellency prevents water and oil from adhering to the surface, thus reducing the need for cleaning.

### Limescale Prevention
Prevents limescale buildup thanks to its water repellent property, ensuring applied surfaces stay cleaner longer.

### Easy Cleaning
Reduces dirt and oil adhesion, making surfaces easy to clean and minimizes the use of cleaning detergents.

### Bathroom and Kitchen Glass Surfaces
Suitable for use on glass surfaces such as shower cabins, cabinet and oven glass.

### Glossy Ceramic Surfaces
Effective on glossy ceramic surfaces such as toilets, sinks, bathtubs, and tiles.

### Stainless Steel Surfaces
Can also be used on stainless steel surfaces such as sinks and faucets.

## Application Areas

### Bathroom
Shower cabins, sinks, bathtubs, and tiles

### Kitchen
Oven glass, sinks, and stainless steel surfaces

### All Glass Surfaces
Cabinet glass and glossy ceramic surfaces
