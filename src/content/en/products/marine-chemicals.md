## Feature Details

### Water Repellent Property
Provides safe driving in rainy conditions and against salt water.

### Easy Cleaning
Reduces dirt and stain presence on applied areas, making cleaning easier and significantly reducing cleaning material usage.

### Long-Lasting Effect
Effectiveness lasts up to 1 year, providing long-term protection on your boat's glass.

### Fighting Limescale and Water Stains
Allows easy cleaning of limescale and water stains on interior glass without using detergent.

## Safe Use

Can be safely applied to the exterior surfaces of yacht and boat glass. Should not be used on other surfaces. In case of incorrect application, the surface should be wiped completely with a dry cloth.
