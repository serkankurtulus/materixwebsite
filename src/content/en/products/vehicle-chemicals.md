## Feature Details

Materix NFC-11 Nano Rain Repellent is a high-quality water repellent thin film coating product with a special nanotechnology formula.

The product improves the driving experience in rainy weather by giving the applied surface long-term water repellent properties. The product maintains its effect on the glass surface for up to 1 year.

Thanks to its features, Materix NFC-11 prevents the user's vision from being restricted, providing clear visibility without using wipers at speeds above 60km/h, especially in heavy rainfall conditions.

It also provides easy cleaning and lower dust accumulation on the applied glass surface.

Materix NFC-11 nano rain repellent is a professional solution carefully produced for harsh weather conditions.

## Application Recommendation

You can use Materix Surface Clean surface cleaning product for surface cleaning before application.

## Advantages

### 4 Season Use
The product provides full protection on the applied glass surface in all four seasons. The product extends the life of both the windshield and the wiper blades by ensuring you don't run the wipers in most rainy conditions.

### Safe Driving Experience
It increases visibility performance and helps you have a safer driving experience.

### Water and Oil Repellent
Thanks to the product, your vehicle's glass surfaces gain liquid and oil repellent properties. Thus, glass attracts much less stain with the water repellent effect and becomes easy to clean.

### Sustainable Cleaning
The product gives the glass chemical resistance, making it easier to clean the applied surface.

When your vehicle exceeds 60 km per hour, you can observe the rain repelling effect on the windshield. With its water repellent and collecting effect, Materix NFC-11 improves visibility quality and driving safety in rainy weather at low speeds below 60 km/h.
